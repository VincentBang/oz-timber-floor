FAQ

Questions about office flooring

These answers help office clients prepare a clearer scope before product selection, site review or quote confirmation.

What flooring is suitable for offices?

That depends on traffic, cleaning expectations, chair movement, acoustic needs, visual finish and whether the office stays occupied during the work. Hybrid, vinyl, laminate and engineered timber can all suit different office projects.

Can office flooring be installed after hours or in stages?

Often yes. Staged installation can help with occupied offices, after-hours work, lift access and keeping parts of the workspace operating during the project.

Do office floors need levelling first?

Many office projects do. Floor flatness, substrate condition, old adhesives and transition heights should be checked before final product selection and installation.

Can you help choose between hybrid, vinyl, laminate and engineered timber?

Yes. Product choice can be reviewed alongside the office type, maintenance expectations, traffic level and timing requirements.

What should I send for an office flooring quote?

Include the suburb, office type, approximate area, current floor, preferred product, access rules and timing. Photos or plans also help if they are available.
