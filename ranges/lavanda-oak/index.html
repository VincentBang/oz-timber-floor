Euro Oak Engineered

Why this range matters in the Oz catalogue

Lavanda Oak blends classic European style with modern durability. Brushed to enhance its natural grain and finished with a UV ultra-matte lacquer and Sherwin-Williams coating, it offers scratch resistance and 48-hour water protection. The T&G boards feature a stable multi-ply core and are compatible with heated subfloors. Matching accessories complete the look.
