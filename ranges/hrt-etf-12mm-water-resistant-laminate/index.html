HRT Laminate

Current supplier pathway in Oz

ETF Laminate Flooring gives Sydney and NSW projects the look of real timber without the higher maintenance or cost that comes with traditional hardwood. Laminate flooring uses a stable HDF core with a photographic decorative layer and a tough UV-protected wear surface, creating a durable floor that works well in modern homes. This 1.2 metre laminate range is built for practical renovations that need strong scratch resistance, straightforward floating-floor installation, and easy day-to-day care.
