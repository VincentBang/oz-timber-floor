Euro Oak Engineered

Why this range matters in the Oz catalogue

Castel Nuovo Herringbone is inspired by classic French design, offering timeless oak beauty for both heritage and modern interiors. Each board is brushed to enhance the natural grain, creating a unique texture and finish. Prefinished with a UV ultra-matte lacquer, it features left and right tongue profiles for easy installation.
