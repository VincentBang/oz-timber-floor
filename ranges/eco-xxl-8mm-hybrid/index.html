Eco Flooring Hybrid

Range overview

XXL 8.0 Hybrid Flooring is an Eco Flooring range for projects that want a larger timber-look board with the practicality of a waterproof floating floor. The range is suitable for Sydney apartments and family homes where durable everyday performance and a clean modern timber look are preferred.
