HRT SPC Hybrid

Range overview

ETF Hybrid Flooring is an SPC hybrid range for Sydney and NSW homes that want a timber-look floor with practical floating-floor performance. Hybrid SPC flooring combines a rigid core, decorative timber-look surface and click installation. Wet-area suitability still depends on manufacturer requirements, subfloor, trims and installation details. This range is made for busy households that want realistic timber visuals, stable board construction and a cleaner installation path.
