Hybrid Planks

Why this range matters in the Oz catalogue

Artisan Hybrid Tile is a tile-look hybrid flooring range using plank-format boards and floating-floor click installation.

It is not traditional ceramic tile. Wet-area suitability, edge details and expansion requirements should be confirmed before final quote.

Use this range page when you want advice on collection fit, current availability, close alternative colours, installation method, floor preparation and whether the product suits the project brief.
