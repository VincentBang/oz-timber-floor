Range overview

Luxury Hybrid 8mm is a hybrid flooring range with a broader timber-look colour library and an 8mm product format. It gives homeowners more colour choice while keeping the product category practical for family homes, rentals and apartments. The range suits projects where a durable floating-floor look and a wider colour selection are important.
