Preparation notes

Before choosing this range

Confirm subfloor flatness, moisture, trims, transitions and access before finalising the order or install date.

For builder, apartment and commercial projects, staged delivery and handover timing should be discussed early.

Need preparation help?

Oz Timber Floor can advise whether floor levelling, removal or installation sequencing should be reviewed before ordering.
