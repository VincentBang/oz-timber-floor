Project fit

Good for

  • Customers comparing laminate colours before making an enquiry
  • Supply-only enquiries where stock and lead time need confirmation
  • Supply + install projects where subfloor preparation is part of the decision

Be careful with

  • Ordering before stock, batch and supplier specifications are checked
  • Installations where floor levels, moisture or transitions have not been reviewed
  • Rooms or sites outside the supplier's recommended use for this range

Preparation notes

Before choosing this range

Confirm subfloor flatness, moisture, trims, transitions and access before finalising the order or install date.

For builder, apartment and commercial projects, staged delivery and handover timing should be discussed early.

Need preparation help?

Oz Timber Floor can advise whether floor levelling, removal or installation sequencing should be reviewed before ordering.
