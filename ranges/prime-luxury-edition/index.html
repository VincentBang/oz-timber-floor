Prime Laminate

Why this range matters in the Oz catalogue

Prime Luxury Edition is a wide-board 12.3mm laminate range for customers wanting a larger-format timber-look floor.

It is best suited to dry internal rooms where the customer wants a more substantial laminate format without moving into engineered timber.
