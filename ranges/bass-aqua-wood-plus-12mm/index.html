Range overview

Aqua Wood Plus 12mm is a laminate flooring range with a large colour library and a thicker 12mm product format. The 12mm format gives the range a more substantial underfoot feel than thinner laminate categories. It suits customers comparing colour choice, product thickness and practical room suitability.
