Questions about Pronto

What type of flooring is Pronto?

Pronto is an engineered timber flooring range. Oz Timber Floor can help confirm current product details, stock and project suitability before ordering.

What should I compare first in Pronto?

Start with colour, board format, thickness and current stock timing, then confirm installation suitability for the project.

Can Oz Timber Floor supply Pronto without installation?

Yes. Send the preferred colour, quantity and suburb so stock, batch and lead time can be checked.

Can Pronto be quoted with installation?

Yes. Include the floor area, current floor type, suburb, access and timing so preparation and installation scope can be reviewed.

Can Oz Timber Floor confirm product information for Pronto?

Yes. Oz Timber Floor can help confirm current product information, stock timing, preparation notes and any relevant project requirements before ordering.

What should I check before ordering Pronto?

Confirm stock, batch, colour, floor condition, trims, transitions and installation suitability before ordering.
