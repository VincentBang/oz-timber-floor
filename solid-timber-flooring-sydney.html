Product advice

Choose the right range, not only the colour

This page helps you compare solid timber options, including raw hardwood, pre-finished boards and Australian species, so you can plan supply, finishing and installation with confidence.

Solid timber can be sanded and refinished over time, but it needs site suitability checks and realistic expectations around movement, moisture and maintenance.

Best suited to

  • Long-term homes, character renovations and traditional hardwood projects.
  • Customers planning sanding, polishing or custom finishing.
  • Suitable subfloors where real solid boards are practical.
