Before ordering

Why confirm before ordering?

These checks keep product selection, stock and installation expectations clear before payment or project planning.

Stock and batch can change

Confirm current availability and batch before payment or installation planning.

Colours vary by screen and sample

Use supplier samples or in-person confirmation where colour accuracy matters.

Floor condition affects installation

Subfloor flatness, moisture, trims and transitions may affect the final scope.

Supply-only and installed jobs need different details

Quantity, access, current floor and timing help Oz Timber Floor respond accurately.
