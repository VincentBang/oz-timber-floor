FAQ

Questions about timber flooring supplier

These are the common details customers ask about before ordering, checking stock or moving into installation planning.

Can Oz Timber Floor supply timber flooring without installation?

Yes. Send the product, range, colour, quantity and suburb so stock, lead time and supply details can be checked.

Can you check stock before I order?

Yes. Stock, batch and lead time should be confirmed before ordering because availability can change.

Can you suggest alternatives if my colour is unavailable?

Yes. Oz Timber Floor can help compare close engineered or solid timber alternatives where current product data and availability allow.

Can supply and installation be quoted together?

Yes. Include floor area, current floor type, suburb, access and timing so preparation and installation requirements can be reviewed.

Not sure whether I need engineered timber or solid timber?

Start with this page if you know you want real timber flooring but still need help choosing the right timber pathway for the project.

What should I send for a faster reply?

Send the product, range or timber direction, approximate area or quantity, suburb, timing and whether you need supply-only or installation.
