Installation service

Good installation starts before the boards arrive

Flooring installation is a sequence of decisions: product choice, substrate condition, underlay or adhesive method, trims, skirting, expansion gaps, stairs, access and timing.

Oz Timber Floor positions installation as a practical trade service for homes, apartments, builders and commercial projects. The goal is to match the flooring choice to the room, not force every product into the same method.

Installation may include

  • Supply + install advice for engineered timber, solid timber, hybrid and laminate.
  • Subfloor checks, floor levelling recommendations and preparation planning.
  • Trims, doorways, skirting or scotia discussions and transition details.
  • Residential, apartment, builder and selected commercial installation support.
