FAQ

Questions about solid timber flooring supplier

These are the common details customers ask about before ordering, checking stock or moving into installation planning.

Can Oz Timber Floor supply solid timber flooring without installation?

Yes. Send the product, range, timber direction, quantity and suburb so stock, lead time and supply details can be checked.

Can you check stock before I order?

Yes. Stock, batch and lead time should be confirmed before ordering because availability can change.

Can you suggest alternatives if my colour is unavailable?

Yes. Oz Timber Floor can help compare close solid timber alternatives where current product data and availability allow.

Can supply and installation be quoted together?

Yes. Include floor area, current floor type, suburb, access and timing so preparation and installation requirements can be reviewed.

Can solid timber be supplied only?

Yes. Supply-only enquiries can be handled when you already know the product direction, quantity and delivery pathway.

Does solid timber need sanding or finishing?

The pathway depends on the selected product and project requirements. If finishing or related timber work is needed, it should be discussed before the order is confirmed.
