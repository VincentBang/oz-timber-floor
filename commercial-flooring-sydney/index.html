Preparation and access

Floor levelling, staged installation and handover documentation

Commercial flooring often succeeds or fails on the hidden items: floor levelling, substrate condition, concrete grinding, access windows, protection after installation and clarity around what is included in the handover.

Oz can plan around after-hours work, lift bookings, showroom access, staged areas and the documentation needed to keep the flooring scope clear for all stakeholders.

Commercial checks before install

  • Current floor condition, adhesives, moisture and level tolerance.
  • Access restrictions, working hours and tenancy or site rules.
  • Product lead time, delivery sequence and staged installation areas.
  • Protection, handover notes and practical maintenance expectations.
