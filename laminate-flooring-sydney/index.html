A practical timber-look floor when the room is right

Laminate flooring is often chosen for hard-wearing timber-look style, straightforward maintenance and strong value in living rooms, bedrooms, rental properties and selected commercial spaces.

The right laminate decision still depends on the subfloor, underlay, room use, water exposure, acoustic rules, trims and current stock availability. Oz Timber Floor focuses on product advice, stock checks and tailored supply or installation support.

Buying checks

What to confirm before ordering laminate

A reliable laminate enquiry should cover the room, the product, the substrate and the installation details.

Room suitability

Confirm the room use, water exposure, sunlight, traffic and whether strata acoustic rules apply.

Subfloor flatness

Laminate click systems need a suitable, stable base to reduce movement and stress at joins.

Underlay and trims

Plan acoustic underlay, expansion gaps, scotia, trims, stairs and door clearances early.

Stock and alternatives

Ask about availability, lead times and close alternatives before committing to a colour.
