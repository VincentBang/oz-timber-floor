Service overview

When floor removal may be needed

Removal is usually the first job when the existing floor is stopping a clean installation or hiding substrate issues underneath.

Before hybrid flooring replacement

Useful when old surfaces need to come up before the new floating floor is planned.

Before laminate replacement

FAQ

Questions about floor removal and stripping

These answers help customers understand what removal work can affect before the replacement floor is chosen or installed.

Can removal and installation be handled together?

Yes. It is often useful to discuss removal, levelling, product supply and installation together so timing and substrate preparation are clear.

Will removal show whether the floor needs levelling?

Often, yes. Once the old floor and underlay are removed, dips, cracks, residue and height problems are easier to assess.

Can adhesive residue affect the new floor?

Yes. Heavy adhesive residue, contaminants or uneven build-up can affect levelling and installation. It should be identified before the new floor is installed.

What information helps a removal enquiry?

Send the suburb, approximate area, existing floor type, access details, whether furniture is present and the new flooring category being considered.
