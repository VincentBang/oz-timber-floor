How enquiry information is handled

Oz Timber Floor collects enquiry details so the team can respond to flooring supply, installation, stock, builder and floor preparation requests.

Information collected

Enquiry forms may collect name, phone, email, suburb, source page, enquiry type, product or range of interest, and the project details supplied in the message field.

How it is used

Information is used to respond to the enquiry, discuss product supply or installation requirements, check availability, arrange site discussions and coordinate relevant flooring services.

Sharing and retention

Oz Timber Floor should only share enquiry details where needed to respond to the request, support approved business systems or meet legal obligations. Site owners should connect the form to an approved handler or CRM and review retention settings before public release.

Contact

Use the contact page to request access, correction or deletion of enquiry details where applicable.
