Can Oz Timber Floor supply hybrid flooring without installation?

Yes. Send the product, range, colour, quantity and suburb so stock, lead time and supply details can be checked.

Can you check stock before I order?

Yes. Stock, batch and lead time should be confirmed before ordering because availability can change.

Can you suggest alternatives if my colour is unavailable?

Yes. Oz Timber Floor can help compare close alternatives where product data and supplier availability allow.

Can supply and installation be quoted together?

Yes. Include floor area, current floor type, suburb, access and timing so preparation and installation requirements can be reviewed.

What should I send for a faster reply?
