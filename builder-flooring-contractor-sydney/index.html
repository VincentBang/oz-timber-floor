FAQ

Questions about builder flooring contractor support

These answers help site teams prepare a more useful flooring brief before drawings, products or timing are locked in.

Can Oz Timber Floor work from builder drawings or schedules?

Yes. Send drawings, room schedules, floor areas, preferred product and site timing so the flooring scope can be discussed clearly.

Can you help when the specified product is unavailable?

Oz can discuss close alternatives based on category, colour direction, construction, lead time and whether the project needs supply-only or supply + install.

Do builder projects need floor levelling first?

Many do. Slab flatness, moisture, old adhesives and transition heights should be checked before final flooring, especially for hybrid, laminate, vinyl and engineered timber.

What should a builder enquiry include?

Include suburb, site stage, approximate square metres, floor plans if available, preferred product, access notes, required timing and whether preparation or removal is needed.
