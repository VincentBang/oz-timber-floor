What to confirm before ordering hybrid flooring

Confirming these details early helps keep product choice, preparation and installation expectations clear.

Check

Floor flatness

Check slab or substrate flatness before installation to reduce movement, bounce or stressed joins.

Review

Apartment requirements

Confirm acoustic, strata, lift access and working-hour requirements where relevant.

Plan

Trims and transitions

Plan door trims, expansion gaps, skirting, scotia and adjoining floor heights before ordering.

Confirm

Stock, batch and lead time

Confirm current stock, batch and lead time before locking in a colour across multiple rooms.
