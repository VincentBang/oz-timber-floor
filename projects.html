Site conditions

Common flooring issues we help review

Good flooring work starts before the product is installed. These details can affect timing, preparation, product choice and the final quote.

Uneven subfloors

Hybrid, engineered timber, laminate and vinyl floors can need a flatter base before installation.

Moisture concerns

Moisture conditions may need review before product selection, preparation or adhesive decisions.

Removal and disposal

Existing flooring, adhesive and floor height changes can affect preparation and replacement work.

Apartment access

Lift bookings, strata timing, noise rules and delivery access should be planned early.

Trims and transitions

Room joins, doorways and adjoining floor heights need practical transition planning.

Skirting or scotia

Wall edges and expansion details should be discussed before installation begins.

Builder handover

Builder projects may need staged work, site readiness checks and coordination with other trades.

Product suitability

Rental, family home and commercial spaces can need different product priorities.
