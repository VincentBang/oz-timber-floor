FAQ

Questions about builder flooring support

These answers help builders and site managers prepare a clearer scope before product selection, site review or quote confirmation.

Can Oz Timber Floor work from builder drawings or schedules?

Yes. Send drawings, room schedules, floor areas, preferred product and site timing so the flooring scope can be discussed clearly.

Can you help when the specified product is unavailable?

Yes. Oz Timber Floor can discuss close alternatives based on category, colour direction, construction, lead time and whether the project needs supply-only or supply + install.

Do builder projects need floor levelling first?

Many do. Slab flatness, moisture, old adhesives and transition heights should be checked before final flooring, especially for hybrid, laminate, vinyl and engineered timber.

What should a builder enquiry include?

Include the site address or suburb, project stage, approximate square metres, floor plans or room schedules if available, preferred product, access notes, required timing and whether preparation, removal or trims are needed.
