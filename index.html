Floor preparation

Good flooring starts with the subfloor

Floor flatness, moisture, trims, transitions, access and removal can affect product suitability, installation timing and the finished result.

Floor condition

Check visible dips, humps, movement and moisture before planning installation.

Trims and transitions

Doorways, adjoining floors, stairs and expansion details should be checked early.

Removal and levelling

Old flooring may need removal or preparation before a new product can be installed properly.

Access and timing

Apartment access, builder handover, lifts and parking can affect the work sequence.
