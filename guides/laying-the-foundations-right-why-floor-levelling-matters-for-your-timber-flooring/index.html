Practical guide

Start with the site conditions

For floor levelling, the right answer depends on more than the surface colour. The subfloor, moisture risk, current flooring, access, room use and project timing all affect whether a product will perform well once installed.

Match the product to how the space is used

Homes, apartments, offices, retail spaces and builder sites place different demands on flooring. Think about foot traffic, furniture movement, cleaning routines, acoustic expectations and whether the space needs a premium timber finish or a practical timber-look product.

Do not skip preparation

Many flooring issues begin before the first board is laid. Levelling, removal, adhesive stripping, underlay selection and transition planning should be discussed early so installation is not forced over a floor that is not ready.

Ask for a human product check

Oz Timber Floor handles supply and installation enquiries through a catalogue model, not online checkout. That means customers can ask about stock, close alternatives, supply-only pricing and whether supply + install is the better path for the site.

What to send with your enquiry

  • Project suburb, approximate floor area and preferred product type.
  • Photos of the current floor, subfloor, stairs, trims and adjoining rooms.
  • Whether you need supply-only, stock confirmation, installation or floor preparation.
  • Any builder, commercial, strata or access timing requirements.
