FAQ

Questions about engineered timber flooring supplier

These are the common details customers ask about before ordering, checking stock or moving into installation planning.

Can Oz Timber Floor supply engineered timber flooring without installation?

Yes. Send the product, range, colour, quantity and suburb so stock, lead time and supply details can be checked.

Can you check stock before I order?

Yes. Stock, batch and lead time should be confirmed before ordering because availability can change.

Can you suggest alternatives if my colour is unavailable?

Yes. Oz Timber Floor can help compare close engineered timber alternatives where current product data and availability allow.

Can supply and installation be quoted together?

Yes. Include floor area, current floor type, suburb, access and timing so preparation and installation requirements can be reviewed.

Can engineered timber be supplied and installed together?

Yes. Product choice, floor condition, preparation and access can be reviewed together as part of a supply + install enquiry.

What product details should be confirmed before ordering?

Board specification, colour direction, quantity, batch, lead time and the installation pathway should be checked before order.
