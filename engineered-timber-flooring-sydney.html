Product category

Real timber character with modern construction

Engineered timber is chosen when customers want a real timber surface with construction designed for greater stability than many traditional solid timber applications.

A premium result depends on the range, board format, subfloor preparation, moisture checks, installation method, trims and site timing. Oz Timber Floor supports both supply-only and supply + install enquiries without turning the page into checkout.

Engineered timber enquiries can cover

  • Supply-only price, availability and alternative oak or Australian timber ranges.
  • Supply + install planning with subfloor checks, trims and access requirements.
  • Floor levelling or preparation before premium timber installation.
  • Builder, commercial and residential project coordination where specifications matter.

Buying checks

What to confirm before engineered timber

Engineered timber should be specified with the site, not treated as a colour-only selection.

Subfloor and moisture

Assess flatness, moisture risk, old adhesive and surface suitability before finalising the installation method.

Board format

Confirm width, length, thickness, surface finish, grade character and colour variation expectations.

Installation method

Discuss whether the chosen system suits floating, adhesive or other installation requirements on the site.

Finishing details

Plan trims, skirting, stairs, door clearances, expansion details and protection after installation.
