Category clarity

Hardwood timber flooring is a real-timber decision, not just a colour choice

Hardwood timber flooring can mean traditional solid timber boards, pre-finished solid timber, raw hardwood, or engineered timber with a real timber surface. Each path can work, but the right choice depends on the property, subfloor, installation method, maintenance expectation and budget.

For Sydney homes, apartments, builder projects and commercial interiors, Oz Timber Floor helps customers compare hardwood options such as Blackbutt, Spotted Gum, Oak, Jarrah, Brushbox and other Australian or imported species before locking in supply or installation.

Sydney service context

Hardwood timber flooring for homes, apartments, offices and builder projects

Hardwood flooring decisions change by site. A house renovation may prioritise character and sanding potential. An apartment may need acoustic planning and lift access. A commercial or office project may need staged work, durable finishes and fast handover. A builder project may need product availability, predictable scheduling and clear documentation.
