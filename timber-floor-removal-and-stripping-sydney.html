Removal service

Old floors often hide the real preparation work

Removal and stripping are often the first step in a replacement floor. Once carpet, timber, laminate, vinyl, underlay or adhesive is removed, the real condition of the slab or substrate becomes clear.

Oz Timber Floor can discuss removal alongside levelling, product supply and installation so the project is planned as one sequence instead of separate disconnected jobs.

Site sequence

What to plan before stripping starts

Removal can affect timing, access, dust control, disposal and the preparation needed before the new floor goes down.

Identify existing floor

Different materials, adhesives and underlays require different removal expectations.

Plan access and protection

Consider parking, lifts, stairs, occupied spaces, furniture and other trades on site.

Inspect the substrate

After removal, check for residue, uneven areas, moisture risk, cracks and weak repairs.

Move into preparation

Use levelling, patching or surface preparation where needed before installing the replacement floor.
